Motor test of p3DX:
	Power on the robot
	wait till a buzer beep sound comes. It indicates that the bot has started
	press motor power button on left side of robot (when viewed from back)
		pressing this button will generate beeping sound 5 times indicating about 5 second time interval
	when this button is pressed within this 5 seconds i.e. befor beeping sound ends, a new beep sound will 
	indicate that motor test is started. then motor will start turning one after other and finally the robot 
	will comer to rest at initial orientation. After few seconds again a beep soud will indicates that motor 
	test sun has ended.
